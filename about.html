Who We Are

WaterAid is a global non-profit organization committed to solving the world’s water crisis. For over 40 years, we’ve been working alongside local communities, governments, and partners to make clean water accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

We believe access to clean water, decent toilets, and good hygiene is a basic human right. Our mission is to make these essentials available to everyone, everywhere — because water changes everything.
